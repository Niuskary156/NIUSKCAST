About EcoTesla

This project was created by Niuskary Salazar as part of a web development assignment.

EcoTesla demonstrates the use of HTML, CSS, and PHP in a functional web application that calculates energy costs and environmental benefits of electric vehicles. With EcoTesla, my goal is two fold: to showcase my technical skills in building dynamic web applications, and to raise awareness about clean energy and sustainable transportation choices. Through a clean design and user-friendly interface, I aim to inspire visitors to explore eco-conscious alternatives that contribute to a more responsible future.

The goal of this project is to promote clean energy and awareness of sustainable driving choices.
